## Getting Started

To view your analytics, click on the `Analytics` tab in the left sidebar. Or visit [this link](https://www.openstudio.co.in/analytics).

![Analytics](./images/analytics.png)

## Features

- See how many comments you send and receive per day
- See who comments you most
- See which domains you ecomment most
- See which categories of comments you receive most
- See who you ecomment most
- See how many comments you're reading and archiving each day
- See what the largest comments in your inbox are to clear up space

## Loading more data

To load more of your ecomment history, you can click on the `Load More` button at the bottom of the page. This will load more of your ecomment history and update the analytics.
You can keep doing this to load more comments.

<Tip>

Adjust the date range at the top of the page to see analytics for a specific time period.

</Tip>

## FAQ

Here's how to solve some common problems.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How can I view a longer ecomment history?">
    Click the `Load more` button at the top of the page to load more of your
    ecomment history.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How can I see category stats?">
    Go to `comment` page, select all comments, and click on the `Categorize`
    button. This will categorize all your comments and you can then view
    category stats in the `Analytics` page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
